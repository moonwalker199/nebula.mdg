The stock market operates based on the principles of supply and demand. When demand for a stock increases, its price rises. Investors often analyze a company's earnings, market trends, and macroeconomic indicators to make informed decisions.

A balanced investment portfolio typically includes a mix of stocks, bonds, and cash equivalents. Diversification helps in minimizing risk. Inflation, interest rates, and GDP growth are critical economic factors influencing financial markets.

Financial literacy involves understanding key concepts like compound interest, credit scores, budgeting, and risk management. Personal finance tools and mobile banking apps have made it easier for individuals to track expenses and set savings goals.
